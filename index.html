About the Candidate

A Son of Mbaise,
Serving His People

Prince Chukwudi Onyekwere is a visionary leader and dedicated son of the Ahiazu/Ezinihitte Mbaise Federal Constituency. Rooted deeply in the culture, values, and aspirations of the Mbaise people, he has dedicated his life to community development, public service, and the pursuit of meaningful progress for every ward in the constituency.

With a distinguished professional background and a proven record of grassroots engagement — from youth empowerment initiatives to medical outreaches — Prince Chukwudi brings both intellectual depth and heartfelt commitment to his 2027 campaign for the House of Representatives.

His candidacy under the Nigeria Democratic Congress (NDC) banner represents a fresh, community-driven approach to federal representation: accessible, accountable, and anchored in the real needs of the Mbaise people.

Legislative Blueprint

Priority Agenda for
Ahiazu / Ezinihitte Mbaise

A clear, constituency-specific roadmap of the bills and interventions Prince Chukwudi will champion in the Federal House of Representatives.

🛣️

Federal Road Rehabilitation Bill

Sponsor legislation to prioritize rehabilitation of the Owerri-Okigwe road and rural feeder roads across all wards in Ahiazu and Ezinihitte Mbaise.

💡

Rural Electrification Advocacy

Champion rural electrification projects through the REA to bring stable power supply to underserved communities in the constituency.

🚿

Water & Sanitation Projects

Push for federal allocation and implementation of clean water schemes for rural wards currently relying on unsafe water sources.

🏥

Healthcare Facility Upgrade

Secure federal health grants to upgrade primary health care centres across the constituency with modern equipment and trained personnel.

💼

Youth Enterprise Support Fund

Establish and advocate for a constituency-focused SME fund to provide capital access, mentorship, and market linkages for young entrepreneurs in Mbaise.

👩‍🎓

Scholarship & Educational Grants

Create a structured annual scholarship programme for students from Ahiazu and Ezinihitte Mbaise at federal universities and polytechnics.

🎭

Arts, Sports & Culture Development

Sponsor bills recognizing and funding the cultural heritage, arts, and grassroots sports development in the Mbaise Federal Constituency.

♀️

Women Inclusion & Economic Empowerment

Advocate for gender-responsive budgeting and legislative frameworks that ensure women in Mbaise are active participants in the formal economy.

🌱

Smallholder Farmer Support

Facilitate access to NIRSAL and CBN intervention funds for smallholder farmers in Mbaise, with particular focus on root crops and cassava processing.

🏭

Agro-Processing Industrial Hub

Champion the establishment of a federal agro-processing hub in the constituency to add value to local agricultural produce and create thousands of jobs.

🌊

Irrigation & Modern Farming Infrastructure

Secure federal irrigation projects to enable year-round farming and reduce dependency on seasonal rainfall for food production.

📦

Agricultural Supply Chain Development

Work with relevant federal agencies to develop storage, logistics, and market access infrastructure for farmers across the constituency.

🏛️

Community Policing Legislation

Advocate for robust community policing frameworks that incorporate traditional institutions and local knowledge to enhance security in Mbaise.

📡

Security Technology Initiative

Champion federal investment in surveillance, emergency response infrastructure, and coordination between security agencies in the constituency.

🤝

Conflict Resolution & Peace Building

Sponsor legislation that codifies and funds community-level conflict resolution mechanisms, leveraging traditional leaders and institutions.

🏠

IDPs & Vulnerable Persons Support

Ensure federal welfare provisions reach internally displaced persons and vulnerable populations across the Ahiazu/Ezinihitte Mbaise constituency.
